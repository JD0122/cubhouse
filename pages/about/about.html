About The Cub House PreSchool

Founded in 2026, The Cub House PreSchool has been a trusted partner in early childhood education for over a decade. Our mission is to create a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can discover, explore, and grow.

Our Philosophy

We believe that every child is unique and learns at their own pace. Our curriculum is designed to foster creativity, critical thinking, and social-emotional development. We combine play-based learning with structured activities to ensure well-rounded development.

Our Staff

Our team consists of experienced and certified early childhood educators who are passionate about working with young children. All staff members undergo regular training and background checks to ensure the safety and well-being of our students.
