Our Programs

Infant Care (6 weeks - 12 months)

Our infant program provides gentle, attentive care in a safe and clean environment. We focus on bonding, sensory development, and early language skills.

Toddler Program (1 - 3 years)

Our toddler program encourages exploration and independence. Activities include music, art, outdoor play, and early learning concepts.

Preschool Program (3 - 5 years)

Our preschool program prepares children for kindergarten through structured learning, social skills development, and academic readiness activities.

After School Care (5 - 12 years)

Our after-school program provides homework support, recreational activities, and a safe environment for school-aged children.
